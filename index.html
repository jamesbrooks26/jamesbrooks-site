About James

I’ve seen this pattern before.

I started as an investment analyst. The job was pattern recognition: spotting structural shifts early, before they became obvious to everyone else. That lens has stayed with me through the 12 years since I came back to Ireland and built my own consultancy.

During that time I co-founded a B2B company and served as COO for six years, building the operating infrastructure from the ground up before we exited. I know what it costs when growth depends on one person being in every room. I have lived it.

When I exited I saw what was coming with AI and went all in. Not a course. Not dabbling. Two years of full immersion, building systems, testing what actually works for a small team. Every wave of structural advantage in business has rewarded the founders who moved early. Outsourcing. Automation. Now AI. We are inside the window right now.

“Every founder will use AI eventually. The ones building with it now will have compounding data, relationships, and pipeline that take years to close the gap on.”
